NewJeans (Korean: Îâ¥ÏßÑÏä§; RR: Nyujinseu) is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of Hybe Corporation. The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin and Hyein.The group pre-released their debut single "Attention" on July 22, 2022, preceding their debut eponymous extended play, which was released on August 1, 2022.

Name

The group's name, NewJeans, has two meanings. It alludes to the idea that jeans are a timeless fashion item and the group's intention to carve a timeless image for themselves.The name is also a word play on the phrase "new genes", referring to the group ushering a new generation of pop music.
